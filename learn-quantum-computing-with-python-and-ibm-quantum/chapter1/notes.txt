Definitions to know-
2Q error (best): The best (lowest) error rate observed on any device edge.
2Q error (layered): Average Error Per Layered Gate (EPLG) is used to measure the quality and performance.
Circuit Layer Operations Per Second (CLOPS): Measurement of how many layers of a Quantum volume circuit can be processed by a QPU per unit of time.
